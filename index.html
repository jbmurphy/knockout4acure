About Knockout 4 A Cure

Knockout 4 A Cure is a community-driven charity dedicated to fighting cancer through the simple, energetic game of Knockout. Every basket made, every player eliminated, and every game played brings us one step closer to a cure.

How It Works

Gather your friends, family, and teammates for high-energy knockout tournaments. Each participant donates an entry fee, and 100% of the proceeds go directly to leading cancer research foundations. Fun, fast, and impactful — knockout for a cause!

Get Involved

Whether you want to participate, volunteer, or sponsor an event, there’s a place for everyone. Together, we can turn every shot into hope for millions.
